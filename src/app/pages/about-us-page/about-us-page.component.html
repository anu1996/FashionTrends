About Us

When people who have high cultural status start to wear new or different clothes, a FASHION TRENDS starts its new journey. People who like or respect these people become influenced by their style and begin providing similarly styled clothes.

FASHION TREND's forecasting resources help predict trends in the fashion industry. Fashion & trend forecasting is the prediction of mood, behavior and buying habits of the consumer at particular time of season. It is no longer a question of finding your markets or consumers by age, geography or income, but looking into how and what they buy, based on their culture, mood, beliefs, occasion & geographic locations, it is also dependent on fashion cycle and plays a major role in introductory phase of recurring fashion cycles.
